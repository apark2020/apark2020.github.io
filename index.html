A deep dive into the widening gap in funding between men's and women's Athletics programs at Columbia University. Using Python, I analyzed Ivy League Athletics programs' financial records published via EADA reports between 2011-2020 and visualized my findings with R and Adobe Illustrator.

Food Gentrification in Morningside Heights

Built an interactive d3 table with data tracking the rapid chainification of the restaurant landscape in Morningside Heights over the last decade. The table shows which restaurant(s) occupied an address near Columbia since 2008, and color-codes each restaurant by the type of business (national chain, local chain, mom and pop stores).

Charged NYPD Officers' Salaries

Analyzed yearly changes in the salaries of 26 NYPD officers with complaints filed against them. Part of a Columbia Daily Specator article awarded finalist in the Society of Professional Journalists Mark of Excellence In-Depth Reporting (Large) category. Data was sourced from NYPD Complaint Data on NYC Open Data, Citywide Payroll Records (also on NYC Open Data), and CAPStat.
